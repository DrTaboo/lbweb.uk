## Description

In this research paper, I focus in on the Enigma Code used by the Germans in World War Two. Specifically, I gloss over Enigma's history pre-world-war-two, the inner-workings, it's operation during the world war, and how both the English and the Polish cracked Enigma using a Bombe. All information sources are cited using Harvard's Cite-as-you-Write, 11th edition. This has taken me over four months to complete and is the first publication I have completed. 

I hope you enjoy it!
